Employer Sponsored Bus Pass Program


Swell travel perks for all your employees.

Convenient Parking and Shuttle


STA's Employer Sponsored Bus Pass Program allows employers to purchase discounted passes. STA will sell monthly passes to the company or organization at a discount of $3.00 per pass provided that the company or organization agrees to pass that savings on to the employees and offer an additional discount of not less than $3.00 per pass. If any employer has a minimum of 100 people who wish to participate in the program, and the employer will make passes available to all employees at no cost, then STA will sell passes to the employer at a 25 percent discount.

  1. When you as an employer provide a discount of up to $3 for your employee to purchase a STA 31-day bus pass, STA will match up to a $3 savings for each pass (a $50 adult bus pass would cost just $44 for the employee).
